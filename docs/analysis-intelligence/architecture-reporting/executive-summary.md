# Executive Summary

Deterministic template text composed from assessment counts, conclusion titles,
coverage notes, and business-impact availability.

Rules:

- no AI
- bounded length
- no unsupported adjectives
- no business claims beyond "not assessed" / enterprise-context present
- conclusions disabled → findings-only summary with explicit note
- insufficient evidence → do not present concerns as confirmed conclusions
- zero findings with sufficient evidence → successful assessment, not synthetic strength
